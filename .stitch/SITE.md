# SmartTask – Site Vision & Roadmap

## 1. Vision

SmartTask is an AI-powered social task and time management platform. It combines intelligent scheduling, social accountability, gamification, and reusable templates to help users build consistent, productive habits. The platform targets students, professionals, and productivity enthusiasts who want a smarter, more engaging approach to daily task management.

## 2. Target Audience

- **Students** managing coursework, study schedules, and extracurriculars
- **Professionals** balancing work projects, meetings, and personal goals
- **Productivity enthusiasts** seeking gamified habit-building with social support

## 3. Key Features

- **AI-Powered Daily Scheduling** – Generates personalized schedules based on priorities, behavior patterns, and mood check-ins
- **Social Accountability** – Share tasks with friends or groups for encouragement and collaboration
- **Gamification** – Streaks, productivity scores, badges, and leaderboards
- **Task Templates** – Reusable templates for common routines (morning routine, study blocks, workout plans)
- **Mood Check-ins** – Quick mood tracking that influences AI scheduling recommendations
- **Progress Insights** – Visual analytics showing productivity trends over time
- **Multi-View Task Management** – Calendar, Gantt chart, and Kanban board views for flexible task visualization
- **Photo Proof** – Upload photo evidence for task completion verification
